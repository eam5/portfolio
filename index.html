Virginia Evictors Catalog

Public information about property owners, or their representatives, who have filed tenant evictions across Virginia. It is intended to aid organizers, policy makers, policy advocates, and service providers by providing insight into who is responsible for the highest number of court-based evictions and eviction filings, and where they are happening.
